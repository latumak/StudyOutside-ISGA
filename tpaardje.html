'T PAARDJE

1,4 km

Adress: Gerard Douplein 1

't Paardje is a modern brown cafe in the centre of de Pijp neighbourhood. It has a massive terrace where young and old locals enjoy a drink in the sun. It is not only a pub, you can also grab a bite from the French bistro menu, with dishes such as snails and oysters.
